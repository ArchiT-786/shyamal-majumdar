REMEMBERING

Mr. Shyamal Kumar Majumdar

A beloved Headmaster, mentor, and visionary whose legacy lives on in every student he inspired.

“A life dedicated to education, growth, and shaping futures — his presence may be gone, but his impact remains forever.”

Late Mr. Shyamal Kumar Majumdar was more than an educator — he was a visionary who transformed lives through education. Deeply respected and loved by students and colleagues, he built not just an institution, but a foundation for dreams.

His Lifelong Contributions

Transformed a broken infrastructure into a Higher Secondary School at Hariharpara, Berhampore, Murshidabad.
Choa B. B. Pal Vidyaniketan grew into a respected institution under his leadership.
Developed school land into a fruit garden (bagan), allowing students to enjoy fruits free of cost.
Established a vocational training institute to develop practical skills among students.
Built a 500-capacity auditorium and a sports field to encourage holistic development.
Mentored a girls’ Kho-Kho team that competed at the state level, empowering young students.

His Vision & Legacy

His focus was not just education, but mindset development — teaching students how to think, grow, and succeed in life.

His legacy continues through every student he inspired, every life he touched, and every dream he helped build.

In Loving Memory of Mr. Shyamal Kumar Majumdar
